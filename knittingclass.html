knitting class

WEEK 1

you'll learn...

  1. casting on
  2. knitting
  3. purling
  4. stocking/stockinette stitch

you'll need...

  1. one ball of yarn (50 grams or more); DK, aran, worsted, or chunky weight recommended
  2. one set of straight knitting needles; size 3.5mm - 5mm recommended (or size recommended on yarn ball band)
  3. a notebook, if you'd like to take notes

WEEK 2

you'll learn...

  1. binding off/casting off
  2. slipping stitches knitwise & purlwise
  3. selvedge edge
  4. seed stitch
  5. garter stitch
  6. yarn over (increase)

you'll need...

  1. one ball of yarn (50 grams or more); DK, aran, worsted, or chunky weight recommended
  2. one circular knitting needle; size 3.5mm - 5mm recommended (or size recommended on yarn ball band)

WEEK 3

you'll learn...

  1. reading a simple pattern
  2. knitting in the round
  3. rib stitch

you'll need...

  1. one 100-gram ball of aran or worsted weight yarn
  2. one set of circular or double-pointed knitting needles, size 4mm
  3. stitch markers
  4. a copy of the Barley pattern by Tin Can Knits

WEEK 4

you'll learn...

  1. k2tog (decrease)
  2. weaving in ends
  3. blocking
  4. knitting a gauge swatch

materials and tools will depend on the topic
