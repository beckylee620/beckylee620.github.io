knitting class

WEEK 1

you'll learn...

  1. casting on
  2. knitting
  3. purling
  4. stocking/stockinette stitch

you'll need...

  1. one ball of yarn (50 grams or more); double-knitting or aran weight recommended (8-10 ply)
  2. one set of straight knitting needles; size 3.5mm - 4.5mm (or size recommended on yarn ball band)
  3. a notebook, if you'd like to take notes

WEEK 2

you'll learn...

  1. binding off/casting off
  2. reading a traditional pattern
  3. slipping stitches knitwise & purlwise
  4. selvedge edge
  5. garter stitch
  6. yarn over (increase)

you'll need...

  1. one ball of yarn (50 grams or more); double-knitting or aran weight recommended (8-10 ply)
  2. the same straight needles you used for Week 1
  3. a notebook, if you'd like to take notes
  4. one circular knitting needle; size 3.5mm - 4.5mm (or size recommended on yarn ball band)

WEEK 3

you'll learn...

  1. reading a modern pattern
  2. knitting in the round
  3. rib stitch
  4. moss/seed stitch

you'll need...

  1. one ball of yarn (100 grams or more); aran, worsted, or chunky weight recommended (10-12 ply)
  2. one circular knitting needle (or a set of double-pointed needles); needle size 4.5mm or larger (or size recommended on yarn ball band); cable length 60cm or longer (if you're using a circular needle)

WEEK 4

you'll learn...

  1. k2tog (decrease)
  2. weaving in ends
  3. blocking
  4. knitting a gauge swatch

you'll need...

  1. the same materials you brought for week 3 (which should now be an almost-finished hat)
  2. one ball of yarn (20 grams or more); aran, worsted, or chunky weight recommended (10-12 ply) -- if you will have some yarn left over from your hat, you can use that
