entwining

INSTRUCTIONS

all measurements can be entered as whole numbers or as decimals (fractions will not work). do not round your measurements; be as precise as you can!

it does not matter whether you use inches or centimeters - just use the same one for all four measurements.

for sweater measurements, it is recommended that you measure a sweater that fits well, rather than entering body measurements.

  1. using whatever needles and yarn you want to use for your sweater, knit a 20-stitch x 20-row swatch
  2. measure a 10-stitch span of the swatch (laid flat) and enter its width into the appropriate box
  3. measure a 10-row span of the swatch (laid flat) and enter its length into the appropriate box
  4. measure the width (not circumference) of the sweater body and enter that into the appropriate box
  5. measure the length of the sweater body from collar to waistband (inclusive) and enter that into the appropriate box
  6. measure the width (not circumference) of one sleeve near the armpit and enter that into the appropriate box
  7. measure the length of the sleeve from armpit to cuff (including the cuff) and enter that into the appropriate box
  9. measure the circumference of your head at the widest part and enter that into the appropriate box
  11. hit "calculate"
  12. mark down the output on your copy of the entwining pattern and get knitting!
