learn to program

step one: knit yourself a 20-stitch x 20-row swatch

step two: measure a 10-stitch stretch and enter its width into the appropriate box

step three: measure a 10-row stretch and enter its length into the appropriate box

step four: measure the width of the middle of the foot you'd like to knit a sock for

step five: measure the length of the foot you'd like to knit a sock for, standing flat, from end of big toe to end of heel

step six: hit "go"

step seven: mark down the output on your copy of the every possible* sock pattern and get knitting!

note: it does not matter whether you use inches, centimeters, kilometers or miles for your measurements - as long as you use the same unit for each measurement, and your measurements are precise!
