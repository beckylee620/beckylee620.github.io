Instructions

  1. knit yourself a 20-stitch x 20-row swatch
  2. measure a 10-stitch stretch and enter its width into the appropriate box
  3. measure a 10-row stretch and enter its length into the appropriate box
  4. measure the width of the middle of the foot you'd like to knit a sock for and enter it into the appropriate box
  5. measure the length of the foot you'd like to knit a sock for, standing flat, from end of big toe to end of heel, and enter it into the appropriate box
  6. hit "go"
  7. mark down the output on your copy of the every possible* sock pattern and get knitting!

note: it does not matter whether you use inches, centimeters, kilometers or miles for your measurements - as long as you always use the same measurement, and are precise!
