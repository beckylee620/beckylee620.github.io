*toe-up, magic loop, two-at-a-time, stockinette, short-row-and-gusset-heel, rib-cuff

1. knit yourself a 20-stitch x 20-row swatch

2. measure a 10-stitch stretch and enter its width into the appropriate box

3. measure a 10-row stretch and enter its length into the appropriate box

4. measure the width of the middle of the foot you'd like to knit a sock for

5. measure the length of the foot you'd like to knit a sock for, standing flat, from end of big toe to end of heel

6. hit "go"

7. mark down the output on your copy of the every possible* sock pattern and get knitting!

note: it does not matter whether you use inches, centimeters, kilometers or miles for your measurements - as long as you use the same unit for each measurement, and your measurements are precise!
